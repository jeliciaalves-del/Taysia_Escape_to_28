Once you land

Hato International Airport (CUR) is about 20 minutes from The Kontiki. Transfers aren't automatic — purchase one through the resort or arrange your own.

Entry & customs

  • Complete the free Digital Immigration Card (DI Card) at dicardcuracao.com within 7 days before you fly — this replaced the old paper form
  • Save or print the confirmation — airlines check it at check-in, and immigration checks it again on arrival
  • Passport valid through your return date, 6+ months recommended
  • U.S. citizens don't need a visa for stays under 90 days
  • Duty-free limit is roughly 200 cigarettes and 1 liter of liquor per adult — keep receipts for anything expensive (jewelry, electronics) in case customs asks

Pack for it

  • Reef-safe sunscreen
  • Water shoes for rocky snorkel entries
  • A dry bag for the jet ski run
  • One nice outfit for a birthday dinner
